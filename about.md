# Carbon SSH: The AI-Native Terminal

Carbon SSH is a next-generation terminal application designed for the modern developer workflow. It blends the raw power of a traditional command-line interface with a premium, high-performance UI and deeply integrated Artificial Intelligence.

## 🛡️ Open Source & Private by Design

**Carbon SSH is built on the principles of transparency and security.**

- **100% Open Source**: Full transparency into how your data is handled.
- **Fully Offline & Local**: Your configurations, session data, and logs stay on your device. Nothing ever leaves your machine without your explicit permission.
- **Privacy First**: We don't track your commands or harvest your data. You are in total control of your environment.

## 🚀 What is Carbon SSH?

Carbon SSH isn't just another terminal—it’s an intelligent workspace. Built with the latest desktop technologies, it provides a buttery-smooth experience for managing local and remote servers, while giving you instant access to the world's most powerful AI models right inside your command prompt.
